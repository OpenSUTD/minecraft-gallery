SUTD Minecraft Project

When the call came to re-invent our Open House digitally, SUTD students answered, coming up with new initiatives in less than 14 days. One of those initiatives was to build SUTD virtually in Minecraft to allow visitors to experience the beautiful campus without needing to come down to the campus physically. Learn more about SUTD Minecraft and other student initiatives.
